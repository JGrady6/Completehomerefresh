Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions we hear most often.

Is there a minimum project size?

There is no minimum project size. Whether you need a single room painted, one closet organized, or an entire home prepped for sale, Complete Home Refresh is happy to help. Smaller projects are handled with the same level of care and attention as full home transformations.

How does the deferred payment work for home sellers?

The deferred payment program is straightforward: I perform all agreed-upon pre-listing services — cleaning, painting, staging, curb appeal, and more — before you pay anything. Payment is collected at closing, deducted directly from your sale proceeds. You only pay after your home has sold.

Conditions: A contract must be signed before any work begins. If the home does not sell within 90 days of listing, a payment plan must be started and continued until the home sells. Given the current Florida market, this ensures both parties are protected throughout the process. *Some conditions may apply.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — Complete Home Refresh is fully licensed and insured in the state of Florida. You can feel confident that your home is protected throughout every phase of your project. Proof of insurance is available upon request.

Do you use subcontractors?

No. Nachelle handles every project personally — no subcontractors, no crews you've never met. When you hire Complete Home Refresh, you get Nachelle from the first call to the final walkthrough. This means consistent quality, clear communication, and genuine accountability on every single job.

What areas do you serve?

Complete Home Refresh primarily serves Hillsborough, Pinellas, and Pasco Counties — including Tampa, St. Petersburg, Clearwater, Largo, Dunedin, Palm Harbor, Tarpon Springs, Safety Harbor, Brandon, Riverview, New Port Richey, Wesley Chapel, and the surrounding Tampa Bay area. For commercial and multi-unit clients, travel across Central Florida is available. View the full service area map.
