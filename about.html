About Nachelle

From a Passion for Homes to a Trusted Tampa Bay Business

Complete Home Refresh was founded in 2018 by Nachelle Kershner — originally operating as Nachelle Kershner's Cleaning and Painting before growing into the full-service home transformation company it is today.

What started as a reputation built on exceptional painting work quickly expanded to meet the full needs of homeowners, real estate agents, and property managers across Hillsborough, Pinellas, and Pasco Counties. Today, Complete Home Refresh offers everything from interior and exterior painting to home staging, custom closets, garage organization, estate clean-outs, demolition, and property management services.

What's stayed constant through all of it? Nachelle personally leads every job. For larger scopes, she may bring one of her trusted helpers to keep things running smoothly and on schedule — but her standards and hands-on attention are always present from start to finish. No strangers in your home. Just Nachelle, showing up, doing the work right, and leaving every space better than she found it.

Over seven years and hundreds of projects later, that commitment remains the same. It's why real estate agents call her for every pre-listing prep. It's why property managers trust her with entire portfolios. And it's why clients come back — and refer their friends.

"Whatever brings you here, the commitment is always the same: show up, do the work right, and leave every space better than I found it. No job too big, no detail too small."

— Nachelle Kershner, Founder

What Sets Me Apart

The Complete Home Refresh Difference

These aren't just business policies — they're the principles Nachelle built this company on from day one.

Above & Beyond Quality

When painting exteriors, Nachelle digs around the foundation and paints below the dirt line — then carefully replaces everything once the paint is dry. This extra step protects against moisture, rot, and peeling at the base of your home. It's something most painters never even consider. It's standard practice here.

Total Accountability

When you hire Complete Home Refresh, you get Nachelle — personally leading every job from the first phone call to the final walkthrough. For larger projects, she may bring one of her trusted helpers to keep things on schedule, but her eye and her standards are always on-site. No surprises, no miscommunication, no hand-offs. That's not just a promise — it's how she operates on every single job.

Deferred Payment for Sellers

Nachelle believes home sellers shouldn't have to spend money to make money. That's why she offers all pre-listing services with zero dollars due upfront — payment is simply collected at closing from your sale proceeds. No financing, no deposits, no out-of-pocket expense until after your home has sold. Some conditions may apply.
